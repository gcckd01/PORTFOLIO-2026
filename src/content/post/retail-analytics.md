### Project Overview
The goal of this project is to optimize the sales funnel for a retail company by identifying drop-off points, analyzing turnaround time (TAT) bottlenecks, and evaluating customer sentiment (NPS) across four major store locations (Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi, Mumbai).

### Key Insights & Impact
**Data Engineering**: Cleaned raw data using Python (Pandas) and Google Sheets.
**Metrics**: Categorized Net Promoter Scores (NPS) and calculated stage-to-stage conversion rates.
**Optimization**: Identified turnaround time (TAT) bottlenecks to improve operational efficiency.
